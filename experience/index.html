Data Scientist / Zalando

Zalando

  • Zalando is a fashion E-commerce company with a revenue of €10 billion and 46 million active customers in Europe.
  • I worked as a data scientist for a competitive pricing team developing pricing algorithms and analyzing sales data and business impacts.

Product-level ROI(Return on investment) estimation

  • The main goal of the project was to estimate the efficiency of competitive pricing at the product level
  • Estimates are calculated based on product attributes(such as price, COGS(cost of goods sold), and inventory level) and weekly sales forecasts.
  • Designed & conducted monthly A/B tests to measure the business impact and accuracy of the model.
  • The proposed model improved profit by 4 million euros per annum.

Cross-price elasticity of demand

  • Analyzed the impact of competitors’ pricing strategy on sales.
  • Identified major competitors by analyzing the cross-price elasticity of demand.
  • Trained a deep-learning model for sales forecast and improved forecast bias for competitive products.

ML pipeline for product matching

  • Built a ML pipeline to match the products crawled from competitors’ websites with Zalando products.
  • The pipeline consists of two main parts, one for calculating multi-modal embeddings and the other for similarity calculation.
  • Pre-trained deep learning models such as fashion DNA, CLIP, and BERT were also integrated into the pipeline for richer embeddings.
  • Keywords: Revenue management, A/B testing, Data analysis, Machine learning
  • Tools: Python(Pandas, Plotly, Scikit-learn, PyTorch), SQL, AWS, Git
